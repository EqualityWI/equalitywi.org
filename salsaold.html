Contributions to Equality Wisconsin directly support our equal rights work.

This website collectively refers to two organizations, “Equality Wisconsin” and “Equality Wisconsin Fund.” Although there is some overlap in the work done by each organization, certain activities must be done by one organization and not the other. This is primarily in the area of lobbying. Equality Wisconsin engages in direct legislative advocacy. Equality Wisconsin Fund’s lobbying activities are limited because donations to it are tax-deductible to the donor. Therefore, most of the lobbying activity discussed in this website is done by Equality Wisconsin. Other educational and community organizing initiatives are done by the Equality Wisconsin Fund.
